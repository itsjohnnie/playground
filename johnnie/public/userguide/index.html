Hey-o! I'm originally Juan, but I go by Johnnie (joh-nee). I was born and raised in Buenos Aires, Argentina, but moved to the US in 2019. I initially relocated to Washington DC, then chased the heat and moved down to sunny Miami, FL with my wife Dani, my kiddo Dante, our baby Olivia, and our lil pup Honey.

In the past, I co-ran an agency called Cruz Barcelona for over a decade from Buenos Aires (Argentina), working with a diverse set of clients across fashion, branding, advertising, illustration, sustainability, editorial, store design, web design, and development.

Previously, I worked at Webflow on their Creative team, developing their brand, event, and web experiences across different mediums while collaborating with many, if not all, teams throughout the company.

Now, I’m a designer at Anthropic’s Creative team, focusing primarily on the Anthropic-dot-com and Claude-dot-com experiences alongside a team of talented people.
