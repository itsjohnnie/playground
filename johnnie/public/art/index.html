Johnnie’s atelier

Art

One small piece a day — algorithms trying to not look like algorithms. Gradients, dither, ink, optics, feedback. After Zach Lieberman’s daily sketch practice.

Every piece is seeded by its date — reload and it’s the same. Open one, tap the artwork, and it’s reborn. Swipe or use the arrows to walk the room.

Made with WebGL and canvas, one file per day. johnnies.life
