# Negatives — sources and rights

All photographs in this folder are in the public domain or released under
CC0. They are self-hosted copies of scans published on Wikimedia Commons;
each deal credits its negative on the sheet (bottom-left) with a link to
the source page.

## Adding new negatives

Add an entry to `NEGATIVES` in `../app.js` (src, author, title, year,
lic, page) and a row here. Only add works that are public domain in BOTH
the source country and the US, released CC0/CC BY, or used with written
permission from the rights holder (e.g. the Joaquim Gomis archive is
managed by the Fundació Joan Miró — permission must be obtained before
any Gomis photograph is added).
