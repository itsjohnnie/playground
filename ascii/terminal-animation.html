A Brief History

                

                    

                        
1960s

                        

                            
ASCII Art Origins

                            
ASCII art emerged with early computer systems and teleprinters, used for creating images with limited character sets.

                        

                    

                    

                        
1970s

                        

                            
Terminal Graphics

                            
Text-based animations became popular in early Unix systems and bulletin board systems (BBS).

                        

                    

                    

                        
1980s-90s

                        

                            
Demoscene Era

                            
The demoscene community pushed the boundaries of terminal animations with creative ANSI art and effects.

                        

                    

                    

                        
2000s+

                        

                            
Modern Renaissance

                            
Terminal animations find new life in modern CLI tools, loading spinners, and retro-aesthetic applications.
